03 · How it works

Four simple steps.
Setup takes a morning. It pays you back that evening.

STEP 01 / 04

Connect your inboxes.

Sign in with Gmail and link your Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking host accounts. Takes five minutes. Replies stay on the platform your guest is already using.

STEP 02 / 04

It reads your history, then answers like you.

On day one it ingests every past message — so it already knows your wifi, your door codes, your tone. Roughly 80–85% of incoming questions are answered without touching you. The rest get sorted and queued.

05 · What it handles

The eight things
hosts want off their plate.

Replies on every platform

Calendars stay in sync

One-tap approvals on your phone
Tricky replies show up as a Telegram notification. Tap to send. Don't tap — nothing goes out.
Per-property details
Wifi, door codes, house rules — stored per unit, so Unit 4's password never ends up in Unit 12's reply.
Sensitive topics stay with you
Refunds, complaints, damage, anything about money — always drafted, never auto-sent.
Every message, logged
A clear timeline of every reply, draft, and approval — timestamped and exportable if you ever need it.
Learns from every approval
Tap approve once and it's remembered — the same question never reaches you again. Combined with reading your full message history, ~85% of questions handle themselves.
Your guest data stays yours
Guest conversations stay on the platforms they came from. We're a concierge, not a middleman.
06 · You're always in control

It drafts.
You decide.

Whenever a message mentions money, complaints, or anything delicate, Host4Me writes a reply — but never sends it until you tap approve. No guesses about refunds, no awkward auto-replies about medical issues, ever.

Read the full policy →
~/host4me/policy.yml
# blocklist — if any match, agent drafts only
blocklist:
- "money"
- "refund"
- "discount"
- "complaint"
- "damage"
- "cancellation"
- "emergency"
- "medical"
- "legal"
- "injury"
- "pets"
- "smoking"

notify: telegram
